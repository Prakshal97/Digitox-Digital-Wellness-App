Take Control of Your Digital Life

Digitox is a must-have app for anyone struggling with phone addiction. Live mindfully, not mindlessly.

Why Digitox?

Apps like Digitox help you take back control of your time and attention. In today’s world, we're constantly bombarded with notifications, endless scrolling, and addictive content. Digitox is designed to reduce distractions, limit screen time, and encourage healthier digital habits. It’s not about quitting your phone—it’s about using it mindfully.

Key Features

  • Notification Blocker: Stay focused by silencing unwanted alerts.
  • App Blocker: Restrict access to distracting apps.
  • Screen Time Tracker: Visualize your phone usage and make informed choices.
  • Digital Balance: Be more present, reduce stress, and sleep better.
